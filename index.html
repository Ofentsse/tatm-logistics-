Who Are We?

TATM Logistics is a South African based company operating in Johannesburg. We provide local courier services for all kinds of businesses that require courier services in and around Gauteng.

Our founders have been in the courier and e-commerce industries for more than half a decade, and they come together with their experiences to ensure that we can provide services that are tailored for our needs and requirements at the best affordable rates.

Our Vision

Our goal is to be South Africa’s leading and most affordable express delivery service, constantly pushing the boundaries of speed and efficiency. We’re committed to providing the best express services at the most affordable rates at all times.

Our Mission

We take the load off your shoulders and ensure prompt delivery to your desired destination with zero hassle, leveraging the latest technology for optimal efficiency. Enjoy the best express services at the most affordable rates always.
