Who Are We?

TATM Logistics is a South African based company operating in Johannesburg. We provide local courier services for all kinds of businesses that require courier services in and around Gauteng.

Our founders have been in the courier and e-commerce industries for more than five years, combining their experiences to provide services tailored to our customers' needs at the most affordable rates.

Our Vision

To be the best and most affordable delivery express service provider in South Africa, pushing the envelope in delivery speed and efficiency.

Our Mission

To take the load off your shoulders and deliver it promptly to the desired destination with no hassle, using the latest technology for operational efficiency.
